Family Tour

Duration


5 Days 4 nights

Here you can take a 8 day tour. Your journey can be tailored to suit you, and you can plan on an additional day or few days on your own

1st Day – Night @ Sigiriya

  • Pick from the Colombo Bandaranayake international airport @. Drive to dambulla When you arrival to sri lanka 2.30 hours drive to dambulla. On the way stop for the meals and having coffee or tea.
  • Visit Dambulla cave temple Dambulla cave is have to climb 45 minute Visit there.

2nd Day – Night @ Sigiriya

  • Polonnaruwa – Sightseeing – Ruins After breakfast drive 1 hour to polonnaruwa Archaeological city. Visit 2.30 Hour there On the way back sri lankan traditional rice and curry for the lunch.
  • Jeep Safari in Minneriya
    • Safari in minneriya national park about 3 hours

3rd Day – Night @ Sigiriya

  • Anuradhapura – Sightseeing – Ruins
    • After breakfast 1.30 hours drive to 1st capital city of anuradhapura Visit 2.30 hours there after lunch back drive sigiriya
  • SPA and health activities
    • After 1,hour drive sri lankan traditional Ayurveda oil massage for the relaxing body.

4th Day – Night @ Kandy

  • Sigiriya – Sightseeing – Ruins
    • After breakfast 15 minute drive to sigiriya for the climb and 2,30 hours up and down.
  • Herble Spice Gardens –
    • After sigiriya 3 hour drive to Experience to see many kind of harble plant and how to make traditional ayurveda oil lunch is in the herble village restaurant
  • Matale – Hindu Temple
    • 30 minute drive from herbal village to very colorful Hindu temple and south Asia highest hindu temple 15 \ 20 minute there
  • Kandyan Dance show
    • 1 hour drive to kandy and you can see longest river in sri lanka after the get experience traditional culture show of kandyan dance show

5th day – Night @ Airport

  • Tooth relic temple
    • After the Breakfast drive to kandy on the batik factory and shop, gems museum and workshop then drive to kandy tooth temple and 1 hour visit there Walk around the Kandy lake ( Buddha statue ,Old Suspension Bridge, Second world war cemetery, Upper lake View point)
  • Botanical garden
    • After tooth temple drive to 30 minute visit botanical garden 2 hour there
  • Tea plantation and factory
    • After botanical garden drive to 1.30 hours and experience to see how make different kind tea And walk the tea plantation 1.30 hours there
  • Train journey to ella from nanuoya Train journey about 2.30 minute


Additional days,

  1. One more day, Ella
  2. One more day, Tangalle, mirissa and unawatuna and beach area
  3. One more day, national park safari and in the night camping site
  4. One more day, or more days trincomale and nilaweli leisure on the beach
  5. One more day, or more days Passikudah leisure on the beach
  6. One more day, Jaffna ( North province )
  7. One more day, Trekking mountains
  8. One more day, Kithulgala rafting
  9. One more day, Hortain plains
