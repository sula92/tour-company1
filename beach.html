Beach Tours

Duration


12 Days and 11 nights

Here you can take a 12 day tour. Your journey can be tailored to suit you, and you can plan on an additional day or few days on your own

1st Day – Night @ Negombo

  • Pick from the Colombo Bandaranayake international Airport Drive to Negombo
    • When you arrival to sri lanka 30 minute drive to Negombo
  • Drive to negombo hotel

2nd Day – Night @ Kalpitiya

  • Kite surfing
    • After Break fast 3 hours drive to kalpitiya You can take the good experience about kitesurfing Lunch is in the hotels
  • Leisure on the beach

3rd Day – Night @ Kalpitiya

  • Kite surfing
    • After breakfast again kitesurfing
  • Leisure on the beach

4th Day – Night @ Anuradhapura

  • Safari in wilpattu
    • After breakfast and drive to 2 hours to wilpattu. jeep safari and get very good experience of the Night camping site on the jungle, sleep under the tent

5th Day – Night @ Passikudah

  • Anuradhapura – Site Seeing – Ruins
    • After breakfast 30 minute drive to 1st capital city of anuradhapura Visit 2.30 hours there
  • Drive to Passikudah
    • After lunch 3.30 hours drive to eastern beach of passikudah

6th Day – Night @ Passikudah

  • Leisure on the beach
    • After break fast Relaxing and sunbathing on the beach Lunch and dinner in the hotel

7th Day – Night @ kandy

  • Herble Spice Gardens –
    • After sigiriya 3 hour drive to Experience to see many kind of harble plant and how to make traditional ayurveda oil lunch is in the herble village restaurant
  • Matale – Hindu Temple
    • 30 minute drive from herbal village to very colorful Hindu temple and south Asia highest hindu temple 15 \ 20 minute there
  • Kandyan Dance show
    • 1 hour drive to kandy and you can see longest river in sri lanka after the get experience traditional culture show of kandyan dance show Dinner is nice place in kandy view point and back to hotel

8th Day – Night @ Ella

  • Tea plantation and factory
    • After break fast drive to 1.30 hours and experience to see how make different kind tea And walk the tea plantation 1.30 hours there
  • Ramboda waterfalls and some more water falls lunch is the nice ‘ Little England ” of nuwaraeliya
  • Train Journey to ella from Nanuoya
    • Beautiful experience on the train with a local crowd . Via the tea plantation and seeing water falls with cool climate about 2.30 hours

9th Day – Night @ Tangalle

  • Safari in Udawalawe
    • After breakfast drive 1.30 hours for the Jeep safari 3 hour safari in udawalawe with wild elephants and more animals
  • Drive to tangalle After safari 2 hours drive to beautiful beach
  • Leisure on the beach
    • Relaxing on the beach

10th Day – Night @ Mirissa

  • Leisure on the beach
    • After breakfast and lunch drive to via the matara to Mirissa Again enjoy holiday relaxing on the beach

11th Day – Night @ galle

  • Whale & dolphin watching
    • Drive to harbour about 20 minute with the breakfast packs and get the boat for the whales Time is depends see whales and dolphins
  • Visit galle fort
    • After Whale watching 1.30. hours drive galle fort and visit fort 1 hour visit there Leisure on the unawatuna beach

12th Day – Drive to Airport

  • Via the colombo drive to airport @…….
    • Drive 2 hours to colombo by highway road to colombo seeing colombo area Drive 1 hour to airport


Additional days,

  1. One more day, Whale watching in Mirissa
  2. One more day, national park safari and in the night camping site
  3. One more day, national park safari and in the night camping site
  4. One more day, or more days trincomale and nilaweli leisure on the beach
  5. One more day, Jaffna ( North province )
  6. One more day, Trekking mountains
  7. One more day, Adams peak
  8. One more day, Kithulgala rafting
  9. One more day, Hortain plains
