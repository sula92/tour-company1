Duration


14 Days and 13 nights

Here you can take a 14 day tour. Your journey can be tailored to suit you, and you can plan on an additional day or few days on your own

1st Day – Night @ Negombo

  • Pick from the Colombo Bandaranayake international Airport Drive to Negombo
    • When you arrival to sri lanka 30 minute drive to Negombo
  • Drive to negombo hotel

2nd Day – Night @ Habarana

  • Visit fish market in the morning
    • After breakfast You can visit and see fresh fish and fish market called ” Lellama “ 30 minute visit there
  • Jeep Safari In Minneriya After fish market drive 3 hour to Habarana Lunch is the habarana village sri lankan traditional Rice & Curry Jeep safari is about 3 hour in the jungle

3rd Day – Night @ Habarana

  • Sigiriya – Site Seeing – Ruins –
    • After breakfast 15 minute drive to sigiriya for the climb and 2,30 hours up and down.
  • Dambulla – Site Seeing – Cave Temple
    • After sigiriya back to hotel for the little rest and again drive 30 Minute to dambulla Dambulla cave is have to climb 1.30 minute Visit there

4th Day – Night @ Habarana

  • Anuradhapura – Site Seeing – Ruins
    • After breakfast 1.30 hours drive to 1st capital city of anuradhapura Visit 2.30 hours there after lunch back drive sigiriya
  • SPA and health activities
    • After 1,hour drive sri lankan traditional Ayurveda oil massage for the relaxing body.

5th Day – Night @ Habarana

  • Polonnaruwa – Site Seeing – Ruins
    • After breakfast drive 1 hour to polonnaruwa Archaeological city. Visit 2.30 Hour there On the way back sri lankan traditional rice and curry for the lunch.
  • Jeep Safari in Minneriya
    • Jeep safari is about 3 hour in the jungle

6th Day – Night @ Kandy

  • Herble Spice Gardens –
    • After sigiriya 1 hour drive to Experience to see many kind of harble plant and how to make traditional ayurveda oil lunch is in the herble village restaurant
  • Matale – Hindu Temple
    • 30 minute drive from herbal village to very colorful Hindu temple and south Asia highest hindu temple 15 \ 20 minute there
  • Kandyan Dance show 1 hour drive to kandy and you can see longest river in sri lanka after the get experience traditional culture show of kandyan dance show

7th Day – Night @ Kandy

  • Tea plantation and factory
    • After break fast drive to 1.30 hours and experience to see how make different kind tea And walk the tea plantation 1.30 hours there
  • Pinnawala elephant foundation
    • After breakfast 1 hour drive to elephant foundation and wash the elephant on the river, elephant meusium and elephant riding there about 1 hour
  • Botanical garden
    • Back to kandy and visit botanical garden 2 hour there
  • Tooth relic temple
    • After the lunch drive to kandy on the batik factory and shop, gems meusium and work shop then drive to kandy tooth temple and 1 hour visit there
  • Walk around the Kandy lake
    • ( Buddha statue ,Old Suspension Bridge ,Upper lake View point ) Dinner is nice place in kandy view point and back to hotel

8th Day – Night @ Nuwara Eliya

  • Tea plantation and factory
    • After break fast drive to 1.30 hours and experience to see how make different kind tea And walk the tea plantation 1.30 hours there
  • Ramboda waterfalls and some more water falls
  • lunch is the nice ‘ Little England ” of nuwaraeliya
  • Relaxing in the hill country

9th Day – Night @ Ella

  • Train journey to Ella from Nanuoya about 1.30 minute lunch is in the ella restaurant
  • Visit Little Adams peak
    • After break fast drive 20 minute for the another view point and up and down 1.30 hour
  • Relaxing in the hill country

10th Day – Night @ Tissamaharamaya

  • Visit Buduruwagala old temple
    • After breakfast drive 45 minute to there and on the way to tissa
  • Jeep safari in yala
    • After buduruwagala drive to 1.30 hour to yala accommodation Before jeep safari having lunch and 3 hour in the jungle
  • (Choose your accommodation luxury camping site or Hotels)

11th Day – Night @ Tangalle

  • Leisure on the beach
    • After breakfast drive to 2.30 hour nice beach of medaketiya relaxing without crowded

12th Day – Night @ Mirissa

  • Leisure on the beach
    • After Lunch drive to 1 hour nice beach of Mirissa relaxing on the beach

13th Day – Night @ galle

  • Whale & Dolphin watching
    • Drive to houbour about 20 minute with the breakfast packs and get the boat for the whales around 6 am Time is depends see whales and dolphins
  • Visit galle fort
    • After Whale watching drive to galle visit galle fort
  • Leisure on the unawatuna beach

14th Day – Drive to Airport

  • Via the colombo drive to air port @…….
    • After Break fast take the highway road to colombo about 2 hours Visit colombo area about 1 hours and Lunch is in colombo After drive to airport @…….


Additional days,

  1. One more day, Whale watching in Mirissa
  2. One more day, national park safari and in the night camping site
  3. One more day, national park safari and in the night camping site
  4. One more day, or more days trincomale and nilaweli leisure on the beach
  5. One more day, Jaffna ( North province )
  6. One more day, Trekking mountains
  7. One more day, Adams peak
  8. One more day, Kithulgala rafting
  9. One more day, Hortain plains
